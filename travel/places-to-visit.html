PLACES TO VISIT

There's something for everyone in Europe, and Ukraine specifically. For those that like the arts, there is some beautiful architecture, and many amazing museums. There is so much rich history there, and you can really go from any angle, either diving into all the history, and learning so much about the culture, or simply learning a little about the country, and seeing some amazing things from their history. For the more adventurous, there are many castles throughout Europe, and Ukraine specifically, that you can either walk around, or often climb around. There truly is an incredible variety of things to do there.

Uzhhorod, Ukraine

Uzhhorod is great because there are so many places to go. You can take a quick trip to the Slovakian border, you can take a quick bus ride to the Nevytskyy castle, and much more. Or, even staying in city there's the castle, a traditional Zakarpathian village museum, and more!

L'viv, Ukraine

L'viv is great for it's architecture. There are so many places to go in L'viv, great food, nice malls, and even just walking around the city and looking at the architecture is enough to spend a few days.

Kosice, Slovakia

Kosice is a nice little town, if you have a day or two to spend. There isn't much to do, although the city center is quite pretty. The main thing to see in this city is the Old Town. Here, there's the Cathedral of St. Elizabeth of Hungary, which is an incredibly beautiful cathedral.
